How to play MathRacer?

  1. Each player inputs their name
  2. Number of players is chosen (2 players is the default)
  3. Number of questions per round is chosen (10 is the default)
  4. 5 or 10 simple maths questions have to be answered as fast as possible
  5. The answer can be inputted by using your keyboard or the on-screen numpad
  6. The first player will answer the number of questions chosen
  7. Their score and time taken will be recorded
  8. The next player will answer another set of questions
  9. Their score and time taken will be recorded
  10. This process will repeat until all players have answered a set of questions
  11. The winner will be determined based on the score
  12. If the scores are identical, winner will be decided by time taken!
