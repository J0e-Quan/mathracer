How to play MathRacer?

  1. Each player inputs their name
  2. Number of players is chosen (2 players is the default)
  3. Number of questions per round is chosen (10 is the default)
  4. Player 1 (Blue) starts first
  5. 5 or 10 simple maths questions have to be answered as fast as possible
  6. The answer can be inputted by using your keyboard or the on-screen numpad
  7. Player 1's score and time taken will be recorded
  8. Player 2 (Red) will answer another 5 or 10 questions
  9. Player 2's score and time taken will be recorded
  10. If there is a Player 3, they will answer another 5 or 10 questions
  11. Player 3's score and time taken will be recorded
  12. The winner will be determined based on the score
  13. If the scores are identical, winner will be decided by time taken!
