How to play MathRacer?

  1. Each player inputs their name
  2. Player 1 (Blue) starts first
  3. 10 simple maths questions have to be answered as fast as possible
  4. The answer can be inputted by using your keyboard or the on-screen numpad
  5. Player 1's score and time taken will be recorded
  6. Player 2 (Red) will answer another 10 questions
  7. The winner will be determined based on the score
  8. If the scores are identical, winner will be decided by time taken!
